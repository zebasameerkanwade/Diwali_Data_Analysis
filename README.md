# 🪔 Diwali Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Diwali sales data to understand customer purchasing behavior and identify key business insights. The analysis focuses on customer demographics, purchasing patterns, product preferences, and sales trends to help businesses improve their marketing and sales strategies.

The project uses Python libraries such as Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand customer purchasing behavior during Diwali.
- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Analyze sales patterns based on gender, age, state, occupation, and marital status.
- Discover top-selling product categories.
- Generate business insights and recommendations for decision-making.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer transaction details during the Diwali festival season.

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis Performed

### Gender Analysis

- Customer distribution by gender.
- Total purchase amount by gender.

### Age Group Analysis

- Customer count by age group.
- Sales contribution by different age groups.

### State Analysis

- Top states based on order count.
- Top states based on total sales amount.

### Marital Status Analysis

### Occupation Analysis

### Product Category Analysis

- Most purchased product categories.
- Revenue contribution by each product category.

### Product Analysis

- Top-selling products.
- Most frequently purchased items.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Female customers contribute significantly more to total sales than male customers.
- Married women in the age group of 26–35 years are the primary buyers.
- Customers from states such as Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka contribute the highest sales.
- Customers working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors are among the highest spenders.
- Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories generate substantial revenue.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus promotional activities in high-performing states.
- Create occupation-specific offers for professionals working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation.
